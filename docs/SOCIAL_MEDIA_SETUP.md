## Engagement Guidelines

### Responding to Comments
- Be helpful and professional
- Keep brand voice: confident but friendly
- Respond within 24 hours
- Thank users for feedback

### Handling Negative Feedback
- Acknowledge the concern
- Offer to help directly
- Take complex discussions to DM
- Never argue publicly

### Partner/Competitor Interactions
- Be respectful of competitors
- Focus on UseClevr's unique value
- Don't engage in negative comparison
- Celebrate industry advancements
